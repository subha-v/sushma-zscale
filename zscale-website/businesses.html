Incentive Types

Common Texas Business Incentives

Tax Abatements

Property tax reductions for qualifying capital investments. Many EDCs offer 5-10 year abatement agreements for job creation and facility construction.

Workforce Training Grants

Skills Development Fund and other programs cover employee training costs. Typical grants range from $500 to $1,800 per employee trained.

Infrastructure Support

EDCs can fund road improvements, utility extensions, and site preparation. Some regions offer land at below-market rates or free for qualifying projects.

Low-Interest Loans

TSBCI and other programs offer below-market financing for equipment, real estate, and working capital. $472M currently available statewide.

Job Creation Incentives

Cash grants tied to new job creation, often $1,000-$5,000 per job. Some programs offer ongoing payroll rebates for high-wage positions.
