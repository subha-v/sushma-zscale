Common questions about zScale Capital and Texas economic development.

The Texas State Bank Capital Initiative (TSBCI) has $472M in available funding for Texas businesses. Currently, 82% of this capital remains undeployed, representing a significant opportunity for qualifying businesses. Source: Texas Economic Development Corporation, 2024.

zScale Capital tracks and indexes 725 Economic Development Corporations (EDCs) across Texas, providing comprehensive coverage of incentive programs, funding opportunities, and partnership possibilities.

zScale indexes 47,312 Texas manufacturers and tracks businesses across all major sectors. Texas exports total $379B annually with 4.7% GDP growth, making it a critical hub for manufacturing, technology, energy, and logistics.

EDCs use zScale to identify expansion-ready businesses, track competitive incentives, monitor regional economic indicators, and connect with companies actively seeking relocation or expansion opportunities in Texas.

zScale updates its business index and economic indicators every 24 hours from official government sources including the U.S. Census Bureau, Bureau of Economic Analysis, and Texas Economic Development Corporation.
