Founder

I built zScale after spending 15 years leading enterprise-scale transformations at Fortune 100 companies—including T-Mobile, Costco, and Nordstrom. During that time, I managed budgets exceeding $500 million and led teams of over 200 people delivering complex technology and operations projects.

The pattern was always the same: good decisions require good data. Yet economic developers, community colleges, and site consultants were making critical decisions with scattered, incomplete information—spending days compiling what should take minutes.

zScale Capital consolidates official Texas state data into one platform. It's built on the same analytical rigor I learned managing large-scale operations: accurate data, clear insights, reliable execution. No guessing. Just verified intelligence that Texas economic development professionals can trust.
